A Note on AI Art

I know AI art gets a lot of hate, and honestly, I get where some of it comes from. I'm coming from an art school background myself, so I understand why people care so much about originality and process. But I don't see what I'm doing here as gibberish or "AI slop." I'm also neurodivergent, my brain just works differently, and a lot of the ideas in my head don't come out easily through a pencil or a tablet the way they do for other people. AI gave me a way to actually get those ideas out instead of losing them. To me, art can push further when you bring a real vision to it, and prompting is its own skill, even if I'm still learning mine.

I'm not the most technical prompt writer out there, but every piece in this gallery started with an idea I actually wanted to see exist. AI became the tool that helped me describe it into something real. The vision comes from me, the tool just helps me see it.
